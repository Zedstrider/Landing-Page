Formula 1
Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing, governed by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It features a series of Grand Prix races held worldwide, where teams and drivers compete using cutting-edge technology and precision engineering at speeds exceeding 200 mph.
